How it works

From documents to grounded answers

Follow the end-to-end RAG flow with intelligent retrieval at every step.

📤

Upload Documents

PDFs or knowledge files are uploaded to the system.

🧹

Text Processing

Documents are cleaned and split into intelligent chunks.

🧬

Embedding & Indexing

Chunks are converted into vector embeddings and stored.

❓

User Question

The user asks a natural language question.

🔍

Intelligent Retrieval

Relevant document chunks are retrieved.

✨

Answer Generation

The system generates a grounded answer with sources.
